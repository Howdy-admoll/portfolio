About Me

Hello, I'm Admoll Adefemi!

I’m an Engineer with extensive experience optimizing processes and delivering impactful results across financial sevice, edtech, healthtech, e-commerce, and government sector. I’ve led initiatives that enhanced operational efficiency, reduced costs, and improved overall performance, including implementing automated systems that reduced customer technical-ticket response times by 25% and conserved company resources. By introducing improved procedures and mentoring teams, I’ve boosted employee productivity and minimized errors. My approach goes beyond problem-solving; it focuses on identifying smarter, quicker, and more cost-effective solutions that drive tangible business outcomes.


Along the way, I ventured into entrepreneurship and co-founded Arhesus LLC, a startup aimed at revolutionizing the edtech space. While it didn't achieve the success I had hoped for, it was a valuable learning experience that taught me invaluable lessons about resilience, adaptability, and the importance of market timing. The journey, though challenging, helped me refine my problem-solving skills and gave me a deeper understanding of what it takes to build and scale a business from the ground up.


When I’m not engineering solutions, you’ll find me immersed in old-school music—Harry Belafonte, Cydney Lauper, Marvin Gaye, Michael.J, and Lionel Richie are my go-to artists while I work. I’m also passionate about exploring new technologies, tinkering with gadgets, and perfecting my cooking skills (I make a mean jollof rice, and pasta!). Whether in the workplace or the kitchen, I’m always up for a challenge
